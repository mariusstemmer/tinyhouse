Location

The Blackforest vacation region covers a total of 11,100 square kilometers in southwestern Germany. The largest low mountain range in Germany stands for different natural landscapes such as long forest mountains in the north, wide meadow valleys in the central Black Forest and the southern Black Forest with tree-free mountain tops rising to 1493 meters.
